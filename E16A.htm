16. Latin English: What do they mean?

  1. Vice versa => the other way round
  2. Status quo => current state
  3. Caveat emptor=> buyer beware
  4. Bona fide => genuine
  5. Quid Pro Quo => a favor for another
  6. Sine qua non => essential
  7. Cogito, ergo sum => I think therefore I am
  8. Non sequitur => It does not follow
  9. Carpe diem => seize the day
