Answer to Q10 .

10. In electrolysis of brine, what would the solution become? Why?

At Cathode (-ve electrode): H+ and Na+ attracted there, but H+ preferentially discharged => H2

At Anode (+ve electrode): Cl- attracted there, OH- blocked by diaphragm, thus Cl- discharged=> Cl2

Net change = Removal of H& Cl

Therefore, remaining solution = NaOH
