16. Learning poetry in English lessons- a letter to the editor

While many students complain about the boredom of learning English poetry,

I see the advantages of acquiring knowledge about poems in English lessons.

In this letter, I would like to draw the readers' attention to the pros of learning English poetry.

Some people may argue that it is always better for students to spend more time on grammar learning and exam practices.

Undoubtedly, practice makes perfect.

All the above activities do prepare students better for different assessments and exams.

Nonetheless, learning a language is not for assessments alone.

It is for communication purposes.

When we leave school, I believe there are many chances for us to use English in our social life,

like going to an opera or writing a poem for special occasions.

Then, the knowledge of poetry will be of great use.
