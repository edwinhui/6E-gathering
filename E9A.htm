  8. 更上一層樓 take it up a notch
  9. 直言不諱 call a spade a spade
  10. 法網恢恢 the long arm of the law
  11. 既往不咎 let bygones be bygones
  12. 英雄所見略同 great minds think alike
  13. 洗心革面 turn a new leaf
  14. 挑燈夜讀 burn the midnight oil
  15. 患難見真情 a friend in need is a friend indeed
  16. 熟能生巧 practice makes perfect
