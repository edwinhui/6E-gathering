20. Correct the following Chinglish.

  1. Slide carefully (小心地滑)=> Mind the floor.
  2. Add oil (加油) => Keep it up!
  3. To abuse plastic bag, please pay money each 2 dollar. (濫用膠袋徵費兩元) => $2 for excessive plastic bag usage
  4. Execution in progress (施工進行中) => Work in progress
  5. Today’s job is very busy. (今天工作很忙)=> It's a busy day at work.
  6. One sail wind follow. (一帆風順) => Smooth sailing year
  7. Hong Kong foot (香港腳) => Athlete's foot
  8. Chok (懶型) => poser/pretentious/snobbish/ostentatious
  9. Inch (寸嘴) => sarcastic
  10. Laugh die me (笑死我) => hilarious
