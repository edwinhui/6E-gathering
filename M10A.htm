Answer to Q10.

10. For y=[(x-1)]^2, translate left by 1 unit, then reflect about y-axis, then enlarge by 2 times about x-axis. What is the final equation?

For translation: y= [ ((x+1)-1)]^2 = x^2

For reflection: y= (-x)^2 = x^2

For enlargement: y= ((1/2)x)^2 = (1/4)x^2
