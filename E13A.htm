13. Vocab question: What does the longest English word Supercalifragilisticexpialidocious mean? (First recorded in 1931)

Answer: Super- above; Cali- beauty; Fragilistic- delicate;

Expiali- to atone; Docious- Educable

=>To atone for being educable through delicate beauty (以精緻美感報答受教育)
