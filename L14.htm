14. The pros and cons of learning English through the Internet

As the American practice argues, the will to learn shall be ignited by interest.

Gaining daily life experience is an important path for learning English,

and that is what the internet can offer.

Students' language sense is cultivated by exposing themselves to audio-visual stimulation the Web has, instead of being indoctrinated.

A picture paints a thousand words.

Disney graphics, Pixar animations and Youtube video clips are impressive means for youngsters to cling to authentic English.

Online digests of books and texts could provide one with comprehensive ideas about the contents.

The uncertainty principle governing the Internet is the strongest appeal for curious adolescents.
