12. In how many ways can 6 persons be seated at a round table, such that A& B always sits together?

By permutation, after grouping A&B, linear combination of 5 groups = 5! x2

However, circular combination leads to repetition by rotation.

Exactly 5 repeats occur for every permutation.

Therefore, number of permutations = (5-1)! x2 = 48
